BOILER SERVICES

TNR Plumbing


At TNR Plumbing, we specialize in providing comprehensive boiler services that encompass installation, repair, and testing. Our team of skilled technicians possesses extensive experience in handling a wide range of boiler types, ensuring that we can cater to the specific needs of our customers. Whether you have a condensing boiler, wall-hung boiler, IBC boiler, or Aquatherm boiler, our experts are well-versed in the intricacies of each system.

Installation: When it comes to installing a new boiler, our technicians are equipped with the knowledge and expertise to ensure a seamless and efficient process. We understand that selecting the right boiler for your property is crucial, which is why we take the time to assess your requirements and recommend the most suitable options. Our installation services are carried out with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that your new boiler is set up correctly and functions optimally.

Repair: If your boiler is experiencing issues or breakdowns, our team is here to help. With our in-depth understanding of different boiler models and their components, we can accurately diagnose and resolve any problems you may be facing. Whether it's a faulty thermostat, a leak, or uneven heating, our technicians employ industry-leading techniques and utilize high-quality parts to deliver effective and lasting repairs.

Testing: To ensure the safety and efficiency of your boiler, regular testing is essential. Our technicians are proficient in conducting thorough boiler testing, checking for factors such as gas leaks, pressure levels, and carbon monoxide emissions. Through meticulous inspections and testing procedures, we can identify any potential concerns and address them promptly, giving you peace of mind and optimal performance from your boiler.


Boiler Services We Offer

  • Boiler Installation: When it's time to install a new boiler, TNR Plumbing is your trusted partner. Our skilled technicians have extensive experience in handling various types of boilers, including condensing boilers, wall-hung boilers, IBC boilers, and Aquatherm boilers. We provide professional installation services, ensuring that your new boiler is correctly and efficiently integrated into your system.
  • Boiler Repair: If your boiler is experiencing issues, our team is ready to diagnose and repair the problem. We understand the complexities of different boiler models and components, allowing us to efficiently troubleshoot and fix any malfunctions. From faulty thermostats to leaks and uneven heating, we employ advanced techniques and quality parts to deliver effective and long-lasting repairs.
  • Boiler Maintenance: Regular maintenance is vital for the optimal performance and longevity of your boiler. Our technicians perform comprehensive maintenance checks, including cleaning, inspecting, and adjusting key components. By scheduling routine maintenance with TNR Plumbing, you can prevent costly breakdowns and ensure that your boiler operates at its best.
  • Boiler Testing and Certification:
    • Safety is our top priority, which is why we offer thorough boiler testing and certification services. Our experts conduct meticulous inspections, checking for gas leaks, pressure levels, and overall performance. We ensure that your boiler meets safety standards and adheres to regulatory requirements, providing you with peace of mind.
