Water Filtration Services We Offer

  • Whole-House Filtration Systems: Trust TNR Plumbing for the installation of whole-house water filtration systems. Ensure every tap in your home provides clean and purified water for your family.
  • Under-Sink Filtration: Enhance the quality of water at specific taps with our under-sink filtration solutions. Enjoy purified water for drinking and cooking directly from the tap.
  • Water Softener Installation: TNR Plumbing specializes in the installation of water softeners to address hard water issues, providing improved water quality and protecting your plumbing system.
  • Filtration System Maintenance: Count on us for regular maintenance of your water filtration systems. We ensure they operate at peak efficiency, extending their lifespan and maintaining water quality.
  • Custom Filtration Solutions: Tailor your water filtration system to meet specific needs. Our experts provide consultation and planning services to design a custom filtration solution for your property.
  • Water Quality Inspections: TNR Plumbing conducts thorough inspections of your water quality. Identify and address issues with our expert assessments, ensuring your water meets the highest standards.
