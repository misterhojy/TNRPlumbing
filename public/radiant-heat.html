RADIANT HEAT SERVICES

TNR Plumbing


Step into a world of unparalleled comfort and efficiency with TNR Plumbing, where we bring you the wonders of radiant heat. Our team of highly skilled technicians excels in delivering top-notch radiant heat solutions for a diverse range of flooring options, including the timeless elegance of hardwood, the sleek beauty of tile, and the robust durability of concrete. Embracing cutting-edge technology and a deep understanding of heating systems, we have mastered the art of infusing warmth seamlessly into every corner of your living space. But our commitment to excellence doesn't stop at the threshold; we extend our expertise beyond your home's interiors to cater to your outdoor needs as well. With our exceptional service, we transform mundane driveways into welcoming paths that melt away the frosty embrace of winter. From luxurious indoor comfort to functional outdoor solutions, TNR Plumbing is your trusted partner in bringing radiant heat into your life.

Radiant Heat Services We Offer

  • Radiant Heat Installation: Our skilled technicians excel in providing meticulous and efficient installation of radiant heat systems. With a comprehensive assessment of your space and careful planning, we ensure seamless integration with your existing infrastructure and proper placement of heating elements for even heat distribution. You can trust us to deliver a reliable and effective radiant heat installation that brings comfort and warmth to your home or commercial space.
  • Indoor Radiant Heating Solutions: At TNR Plumbing, we specialize in indoor radiant heating solutions for residential and commercial buildings. Our experienced technicians are well-versed in installing radiant heat systems that seamlessly integrate with your existing heating infrastructure. Whether it's a new construction project or a retrofitting endeavor, we ensure efficient and customized temperature control through zoning options. With our expertise, you can experience the cozy comfort of radiant heat throughout your indoor spaces, tailored to your specific needs and preferences.
  • Types of Floor We Service: Tile, Wood, Marble, Concrete, Stone, Porcelain Tile, Laminate
  • Outdoor Radiant Heating Solutions: Our outdoor radiant heating solutions are designed to enhance your outdoor living spaces, ensuring comfort and safety throughout the year. Whether it's driveways, walkways, or outdoor patios and porches, our skilled technicians expertly install radiant heat systems that melt away snow and ice, creating safe and welcoming surfaces. With energy-efficient options and precise temperature control, we transform your outdoor areas into cozy retreats, allowing you to enjoy the beauty of nature without the inconvenience of harsh weather conditions.
  • Repair and Maintenance: Our experienced technicians specialize in diagnosing and resolving issues with existing radiant heat systems. From troubleshooting and identifying faulty components to performing repairs and replacements, we ensure your system is back up and running smoothly. Regular maintenance is also essential for optimal performance, and our team provides comprehensive checks, cleaning, and adjustments to keep your radiant heat system operating efficiently and prolong its lifespan. With our repair and maintenance services, you can rely on us to maintain the comfort and functionality of your radiant heat system.
